About Flor Ever

At Flor Ever, we are dedicated to bringing the timeless beauty of nature into your life through the art of flowers. Established in 2021, we passionately provide the freshest and most exquisite blooms to our valued customers.

Our Story

Our journey began in a small shop where our founder, Queenie Raet, a passionate florist, envisioned creating an oasis of floral elegance in our town. Our commitment to quality, creativity, and exceptional service has allowed us to blossom and expand, serving our community with love and dedication.

Our Mission

Flor Ever is more than a flower shop; it's a place where emotions are expressed through the language of flowers. Our mission is to curate and deliver arrangements that speak volumes, whether it's celebrating life's special moments, bringing comfort in times of need, or simply adding joy to everyday life.
